## FAQ

### Hands-free mode and microphone access

Hands-free mode is optional and off until you enable it in Settings. When enabled during a review session, the app uses the microphone and speech recognition only to listen for short voice commands—for example, say **flip** to reveal the answer, **good** to rate a card, or **end** to finish the session. Audio is processed on your device to recognize commands; it is not recorded, stored, or sent to our servers.

### Is my data stored online?

No. Your flashcards, review history, and settings are stored only on your device. We do not operate servers that collect your study data.

### How do I import decks?

You can create cards in the app, paste or import CSV files, or import Anki `.apkg` decks from the deck creation or deck detail screens. Imports are processed locally on your device.

### How do I delete my data?

Delete individual decks inside the app (which removes associated cards and review history), or uninstall the app to remove all local data from your device.
